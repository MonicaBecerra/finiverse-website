Cookie Policy

Short version: we use as few cookies as possible and none of them are for advertising.

Last updated: June 2026

TL;DR: This website uses a small number of cookies to remember your language preference and understand basic traffic. We use no advertising cookies, no retargeting, and no cookies that track you across other websites.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember things about you — like your language preference or whether you've dismissed a banner — across page loads and visits.

The Finiverse iOS app itself does not use browser cookies. This policy applies only to this website (monicabecerra.github.io/finiverse-website).

LocalStorage vs cookies

Our language preference (finiverse-lang) is actually stored in your browser's localStorage, not as a traditional cookie. It works the same way functionally — it remembers your choice across visits — but it's only readable by this website and is never sent to any server.

Third-party services

This website uses the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts — Loads the Inter typeface. Google may log requests to their CDN. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — Processes contact and waitlist form submissions. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — Links to download the app. If you click through, Apple's own cookies and policies apply.

How to control cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here are direct links for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Clearing localStorage will reset your language preference to English. Other than that, there are no functional consequences for refusing non-essential cookies on this site.

Changes to this policy

If we ever start using new cookies or third-party services, we'll update this page and note the date at the top. We won't add advertising or tracking cookies without being transparent about it.
