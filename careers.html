Careers

We're small. The problem isn't.

Finiverse is built by a tiny team that genuinely believes personal finance should work for every brain, not just neurotypical ones. If that matters to you too, keep reading.

Let's be honest with you.

We're not a startup with 40 engineers, a ping-pong table, and a Series A. We're a tiny, independent team, right now that mostly means Monica, the founder, building something real from scratch.

We don't have formal job listings right now. What we do have is a clear mission, a live app on the App Store, and people reaching out who want to help build it.

If you see yourself in our values and want to build with us, even in a small, early capacity, we'd love to hear from you.

What we care about.

🧠

Neurodivergent-first

We design for ADHD, anxiety, dyscalculia, autism, then it works great for everyone else. Not the other way around.

🚫

No shame, ever

Finance apps love making people feel bad. We don't. Every word we write, in the app, in Sam's responses, on this site, is judgment-free.

🔒

Privacy by default

Your financial data is deeply personal. We don't sell it. We don't analyze it for ads. We build privacy in, not on.

🐢

Sustainable pace

We're not racing to ship features. We're building something that lasts. We don't glorify overwork, we've seen what burnout does.

💬

Direct communication

No politics. No vague feedback. We say what we mean, we assume good intent, and we disagree openly when something's not right.

🌍

Remote & async

We build remotely by default. That means flexibility, and trusting people to own their work without constant check-ins.

Who we'd love to hear from.

Remote Part-time / Contract

A therapist, counsellor, or researcher who wants to consult on how Finiverse approaches emotional check-ins, spending triggers, and the psychology of money. Even occasional input matters.

Remote Advisory / Consulting
