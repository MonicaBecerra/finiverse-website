App description

One sentence

Finiverse is the personal finance app built for neurodivergent minds, with Sam AI, emotional check-ins, gamification, and zero judgment.

Short paragraph

Finiverse is a personal finance app designed from the ground up for neurodivergent users, people with ADHD, anxiety, dyslexia, dyscalculia, Down syndrome, and more. Unlike traditional finance apps that overwhelm with data, Finiverse combines mindful expense tracking with an emotional check-in system, a dopamine-aware wishlist, AI-powered insights from Sam (built on Claude by Anthropic), and a full gamification layer that rewards financial consistency. Available on iOS with English and Spanish support.
