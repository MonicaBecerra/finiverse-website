What Finiverse does

Everything your finances need.
Nothing they don't.

Simple, visual, and designed for the way neurodivergent brains actually work.

💸

Track Income & Expenses

Log every transaction with categories, dates, and notes. Get a clear picture of your cash flow without the overwhelm.

❤️

Emotion × Finance

Record how you feel when you spend. Discover patterns between your emotions and your money — something no other app does.

🎯

Savings Goals & Wishes

Set goals, track progress visually, and use the Pause feature to hold a wish until you're ready — no pressure, your pace.

🃏

Cards & Accounts

Manage your credit and debit cards, track statements, and see balances. Multi-currency supported.

📊

Visual Analytics

Charts, trends, and breakdowns that make sense at a glance. See where your money goes — and how you feel about it.

🏆

Levels & Achievements

Build financial habits through gamification. Earn XP, level up, and celebrate your wins — every step counts.
