The Problem

Finance apps were built for
one type of brain.

Neurotypical brains. Everyone else gets overwhelmed, avoids the app, and gives up. Not because they're bad with money, because the app wasn't designed for them.

🧠

ADHD

Impulsive spending, time blindness, and dopamine-driven purchases make every standard budget app feel like a trap.

💚

Financial Anxiety

Checking a bank account triggers dread. Dashboards with red numbers cause panic. Most users avoid apps that make them feel worse.

🔢

Dyscalculia

Numbers don't stick. Percentages cause panic. Spreadsheet-style interfaces are actively harmful for people who process numbers differently.

The Solution

Finiverse is
built different. By design.

Every other finance app ✦ Finiverse
Generic categories, built for spreadsheet thinkers Emotion-tagged transactions, mood tracks with money
