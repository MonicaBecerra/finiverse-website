Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel the Sam AI subscription?

The Sam AI subscription is managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions and cancel there. No cancellation fees.

Is Sam AI actually an AI?

Yes! Sam is powered by Claude AI language models (by Anthropic) with full access to your financial context — your records, emotions, goals, and spending patterns. Sam is not a chatbot with scripted answers.

Is my financial data safe?

Yes. All data is stored on Google Firebase with enterprise-grade security, encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

What does Sam AI NOT do?

Sam AI does not connect to your bank accounts, does not make payments, and does not provide certified financial advice. Sam is an informational AI companion — always consult a professional for major financial decisions.
