Let's build together.

Finiverse is designed to complement the work of professionals and organizations who serve neurodivergent communities. We're looking for partners who share our belief: that every mind deserves clear, calm financial tools.

Why Finiverse

Built with the community in mind.

🧠
Neurodivergent-first

Designed for ADHD, dyslexia, autism, anxiety, and more, not adapted from neurotypical tools.

🤖
AI that understands context

Sam knows the full financial and emotional history of each user, not a generic chatbot.

🌍
Global & multilingual

Available in English and Spanish. Multi-currency support. Designed for users anywhere.

🔒
Privacy-first

No bank connections. No data selling. Encrypted on Google Firebase. Your users stay safe.

🎮
Engaging for everyone

XP, levels, seasonal missions, and avatars make financial tracking feel rewarding, not overwhelming.

💜
Zero judgment

Sam never shames or lectures. Every interaction is designed to feel safe, supportive, and empowering.

Partnership types

Who we partner with.

🧠
Mental Health Professionals

Therapists, psychologists, and coaches who work with clients dealing with financial anxiety, impulsive spending, money avoidance, or financial trauma. Finiverse provides a safe daily tracking space and gives Sam a way to offer gentle, evidence-informed reflections between sessions.

Therapists Psychologists ADHD coaches Financial therapists
🎓
Universities & Schools

Educational institutions that want to support students with ADHD, dyslexia, and anxiety in building financial independence alongside their studies. Finiverse works across any currency and any country, making it ideal for international student populations.

Student support centers Disability offices Financial literacy programs
🏢
Employers & HR Teams

Financial stress is one of the leading causes of reduced productivity and workplace anxiety. Offering Finiverse as an employee wellness benefit, especially in teams with neurodivergent members, is a meaningful and low-cost investment in employee wellbeing.

Employee wellness Neurodiversity inclusion HR benefits
🌐
Neurodivergent Communities & Advocacy Groups

ADHD communities, autism organizations, Down syndrome advocacy groups, sensory processing foundations, we'd love to co-create content, resources, and educational material that directly serves your community members. We believe the people closest to the problem are the best partners to solve it.

ADHD Autism Down syndrome Sensory processing Dyslexia
