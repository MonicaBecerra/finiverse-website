🤖 Sam AI

Sam analyzes your spending, detects emotional spending patterns, and gives personalized suggestions based on your complete financial history and emotional data. Ask Sam anything, 'Did I overspend this month?', 'What's my biggest expense category?', 'Help me save $200 in 30 days', or even 'I'm feeling overwhelmed, where do I start?' Sam is powered by Claude AI (Anthropic) and never judges.
Sam requires an internet connection to generate AI responses. You can still record expenses, view your balance, and access analytics while offline, Sam just won't be available for chat.
Go to Settings → Sam → Response style. You can choose from: Brief (short and direct), Calm (gentle tone, designed for anxiety), Detailed (full analysis), or Motivating (upbeat and encouraging). You can switch anytime, even mid-conversation.
Sam's responses are for informational and reflective purposes only, not professional financial, tax, legal, or investment advice. Sam helps you understand your own patterns and make more informed personal decisions. Always consult a qualified professional for significant financial decisions.

🛡️ Privacy & Security

Yes. Your data is linked only to your account and is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising. It's stored securely using Google Firebase with enterprise-grade encryption (GDPR and SOC 2 compliant). We do not store your data on our own servers.
Yes. Enable biometric authentication in Settings → Security → Face ID / Touch ID. Your financial data stays locked behind Face ID until you authenticate.
Go to Settings → Security → Delete account. The process includes a confirmation step to protect against accidental deletion. All your data is permanently erased and cannot be recovered.

💰 Plans & Billing

Yes · Finiverse is completely free to download and use. The Free plan includes unlimited expense and income recording, 5 mood check-ins per month, basic Sam AI access, goals tracking, and the full gamification system (XP, levels, seasonal events).
Monthly prices (USD): Early Bird, $2.99/mo · Premium, $9.99/mo · Sam AI, $14.99/mo. Annual plans save approximately 20%. Prices are shown in your local currency in the App Store.
To change plans: go to Settings → My Plan inside the app. To cancel: go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Finiverse → Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, you keep access until then.
Everything in Premium plus: unlimited Sam AI conversations, personalized financial context awareness, what-if simulations, early access to new features, 6+ animated avatars with voice, exclusive seasonal events, and the Sam Insider badge.
