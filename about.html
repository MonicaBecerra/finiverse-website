Our story

Built for minds that think differently.

Finiverse started with a simple belief: everyone deserves tools that work with their brain, not against it.

The problem we saw

Finance apps weren't
made for everyone.

Most personal finance apps are overwhelming — dense spreadsheets, complex menus, and zero emotional context. For neurodivergent users, this often means giving up entirely.

We built Finiverse because we believe the relationship between emotions and money is the most important insight missing from every other app. When you understand how you feel when you spend, you understand your patterns — and you can change them.

💡

Our insight

Spending is emotional. But no app tracked both the transaction AND how you felt making it — until now.

🧠

Our users

People with ADHD, dyslexia, Down syndrome, anxiety, and anyone who felt left behind by traditional finance tools.
