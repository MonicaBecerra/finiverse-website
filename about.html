Our story

Built for minds that think differently.

Finiverse started with a simple belief: everyone deserves tools that work with their brain, not against it.

The problem we saw

Finance apps weren't
made for everyone.

Most personal finance apps are overwhelming, dense spreadsheets, complex menus, and zero emotional context. For neurodivergent users, this often means giving up entirely.

We built Finiverse because we believe the relationship between emotions and money is the most important insight missing from every other app. When you understand how you feel when you spend, you understand your patterns, and you can change them.

💡

Our insight

Spending is emotional. But no app tracked both the transaction AND how you felt making it, until now.

🧠

Our users

People with ADHD, dyslexia, Down syndrome, anxiety, and anyone who felt left behind by traditional finance tools.

Why emotions + finance

Built from
lived experience.

One of Finiverse's co-founders has ADHD and anxiety. For years, opening a banking app triggered a spiral, walls of red numbers, budget alerts, zero context. It didn't help. It hurt.

When she noticed her spending spikes always happened during her most anxious weeks, the insight was clear: the missing feature wasn't another budget category, it was emotional context. That realization became the core of Finiverse.

“
M
Monica
Co-founder, Finiverse
“For years I thought I was just bad with money. It took therapy to understand that my emotions were running every financial decision I made, and that my brain works differently, not worse, just differently. That realization changed everything. I built Finiverse to give people like us real visibility into our finances and a genuine path to economic independence. Because we deserve support, not shame.”
