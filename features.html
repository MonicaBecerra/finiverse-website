Your AI that actually knows you.

💜 Emotional Check-in

Your feelings are financial data.

Every day, log your mood, energy, focus, stress level, and sleep. Sam connects those emotional signals with your spending, and shows you patterns you'd never notice on your own.

  • ✓
    Mood, focus, energy, stress, sleep, all in one daily check-in
  • ✓
    Apple Watch sync, sleep and heart rate imported automatically
  • ✓
    Sam links emotions to spending: "You tend to spend more on anxious Fridays"
  • ✓
    No wrong answers. No pressure.

  • ✓
    Expenses, Income, Loans, Goals, 4 record types in one tap
  • ✓
    Add an emotion tag to every record
  • ✓
    Smart categories with color-coded icons
  • ✓
    Filter by Today, This Week, This Month, or All time
  • ✓
    Search and edit any record at any time

✨ Wishlist

Want it? Wait first.

The Finiverse Wishlist is designed to break the dopamine-buy cycle. Instead of purchasing impulsively, you add items to your list and set a waiting period. If you still want it after that time, it's a real want, not a reaction.

  • ✓
    Add anything you want to buy, before buying it
  • ✓
    Set a personal waiting period (24h, 3 days, 1 week)
  • ✓
    Sam reminds you when the wait is over, and asks if you still want it
  • ✓
    Especially helpful for ADHD and impulsive spending patterns
