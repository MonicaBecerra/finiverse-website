Questions

How do I cancel?

Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Cancel anytime, no fees.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

What is Sam AI, really?

Sam is powered by Claude AI (Anthropic) and knows your full financial context, your records, emotions, and goals. It's not a scripted chatbot. Note: Sam does not give certified financial advice.

Is there a student or family discount?

Not yet, but it's on our roadmap. Join the waitlist to be the first to know.
