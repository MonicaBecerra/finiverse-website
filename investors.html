Market opportunity

A $50B+ market
waiting to be served.

Over 1 billion people worldwide have some form of neurodivergence, ADHD, dyslexia, autism, Down syndrome, and more. The personal finance software market is $1.57B globally and growing at 5.6% CAGR. Yet no major player targets neurodivergent users.

  • → 1 in 5 people have ADHD, dyslexia, autism, or related conditions
  • → Neurodivergent users struggle more with financial management, and know it
  • → No major finance app positions itself for this audience, first-mover advantage
